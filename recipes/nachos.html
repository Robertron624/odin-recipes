Nachos with Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Corn nachos

Steps

  1. Wash the tomatoes, onion and cilantro, and chop finely..
  2. Peel the avocado and cut the flesh into squares.
  3. Mix the avocado flesh, cilantro and vegetables in a mortar. Add the lime juice to taste with a little salt and a few drops of oil.
  4. Serve the guacamole in a bowl along with the nachos.
  5. If you want, you can add a little tabasco to the mixture to give it a spicy touch.
