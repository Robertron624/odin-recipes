Cuban Rice!

Ingredients

  • 1,500 grams rice
  • 2 coconuts
  • 1 large white onion
  • 3 red, green and yellow bell peppers, red, green and yellow
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 package baconettes
  • 1 package ham
  • 1 large chicken breast
  • 700 grams pork
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Grate or blend the coconuts, strain the coconut milk, with this pure milk prepare the mitote.
  2. Myth you sauté until caramelise, as if it were a supercoconut and without burning it add the other coconut milk.
  3. This milk, before boiling, salt and sugar are added and tested to verify that the flavor is balanced.
  4. Then, with another little bit of coconut milk, blend part of the raisins and add it where the rice is going to be prepared.
  5. When the rice dries, cover and lower the heat.
  6. Then in another bowl add the chopped chicken, chopped ham, chopped and sautéed bacon, chopped pork in strips, well marinated and sautéed, all this is sautéed in oil with soy sauce.
  7. Once the rice is ready, mix it with the sautéed meats and if it is to your liking, add whole raisins and mix.
  8. Finally, serve the rice and decorate with the peppers cut into strips and the carrot cut into small cubes.
