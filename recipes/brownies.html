Brownies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200 g. unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs L
  • 225 g of normal or powdered sugar
  • 30 g. unsweetened cocoa powder (I recommend 70% cocoa powder)
  • ½ tsp. vanilla essence
  • 120 g wheat flour
  • Optional (in this case not included): 50 g. walnuts, chopped
